AGENDA

17TH ANNUAL LONDON IMAGING DISCOVERY DAY (LIDD)
DARRYL J. KING STUDENT LIFE CENTRE – KING’S UNIVERSITY COLLEGE
 THURSDAY, JUNE 9, 2022

Overall Learning Objectives:
By the end of this program, participants will be able to:

  1. Recognize the research being conducted trainees, residents, and graduate students.
  2. Identify opportunities for collaboration between residents, graduate students, technologists, nurses and admin to improve patient outcomes.
  3. Appraise and discuss the scientific presentations with respect to methodology and clinical applicability.
  4. Identify emerging fields in Medical Imaging and consider their impact on clinical practice.

Dr. Gabor Fichtinger

Introductions to Frugal Technologies.
Point-of-Care-Ultrasound Guided Therapies and Interventions in the Global Health Context

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:
Objective 1: Review the concept of point-of-care ultrasound image guided therapy (POCUS IGT)
Objective 2: Describe the role of POCUS GGT in addressing pressing problems in global health
Objective 3: Recognize current efforts in the implementation of POCUS IGT programs in West Africa

1:40 pm

Dr. Terry Peters & Dr. Elvis Chen

The role of Augmented and Virtual Reality in Frugal Imaging Technologies

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:
Objective 1: Define the terms virtual, augmented, and mixed reality
Objective 2: Name different classes of application of virtual and augmented reality
Objective 3: Recognize how AR and VR can impact frugal imaging technologies

Empowering Frugal Innovation

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:
Objective 1: Identify approaches to enhancing scientific research capacity to enhance frugal innovations
Objective 2: Review cost-effective models to build innovation capacity in health, biomedical engineering, etc. in the developing world
Objective 3: Summarize a biomedical engineering project between Western and a university in Uganda

Dr. Aaron Fenster

Development of AI-Enhanced Point-of-Care Frugal Ultrasound

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:
Objective 1: Review 3D ultrasound imaging applications

Objective 3: Compare integrated 3D and 2D ultrasound and machine learning tools

Dr. Udunna Anazodo

A Framework for addressing Global Health challenges in Radiology

By the end of this session, participants will be able to:
Objective 1: Describe how to evaluate the medical imaging needs in low-resourced settings, especially in low and middle income countries
Objective 2: State how access to healthcare and delivery is defined in low-resourced setting
Objective 3: Recognize how to address low access to high value medical imaging care through building local capacity
