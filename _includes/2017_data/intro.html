London Imaging Discovery Day 2022

After two years of virtual meetings, London Imaging Discovery Day is back in person, in June, 2022!

Following the success of the London Imaging Discovery Day 2019, we would like to encourage you to participate and make LIDD 2022 even better!

In 2019 we had our biggest LIDD yet, with over 300 attendees! This year, we will once again be utilizing the outstanding conference space at Kings University College, in order to accommodate our learners, imaging scientists, technologists, nurses, administrators, Medical Imaging faculty and Industry partners.

We are pleased to announce the following date for your diary:

When: Thursday, June 9, 2022, Full Day
Where: Darryl J. King Student Life Centre, King’s University College

The facility has the following:
  • Main auditorium that accommodates up to 476 people
  • Purpose built presentation rooms for the “theme based oral presentations”
  • Central display area for Industry partners
  • Ample, free parking.
Highlights of LIDD 2019
  • 70+ theme based oral presentations covering 9 themes in Medical Imaging
  • 4 invited lecturer presentations, plus a keynote speaker
Peer reviewed scientific awards:
  • Magda Cum Laude
  • Certificate of Merit
  • Honorable Mention
Inspirational awards
  • Rankin/Singh Award — Research
  • Jason Ashley Memorial Award
  • Mark Landis Annual Award
  • Chair's Award for Inspiration
  • Annual Award for Academic Excellence
  • Lifetime Achievement Award
Kindly mark the date in your calendar.
